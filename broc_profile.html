Who am I?

I am Broc Edson. I program for video games. I am currently a student at Bradley University, but I will be graduating in 2022. I have had a lifelong passion for video games and the process of making them. Over the years I have been involved in many game projects, including Lingering Legacy, Disaster Golf, and many game jams and personal projects.

The hobbies that I enjoy include playing video games and D&D, drawing, listening to music, doing improv, and reading books.
